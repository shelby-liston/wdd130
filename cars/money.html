Saving Money

You're likely asking yourself: How much gas money will I save if I switch to a fuel-efficient vehicle? You're in the right place!

Thank you California Center for Jobs & the Economy for this graph! https://centerforjobs.org/ca/energy-reports/california-energy-price-data-for-april-2022

As shown in this graph above, "Fuel Cost Price Spikes: CA vs. Rest of US," it visualizes the difference in gas prices in California and the rest of the United States. It is very clear that gas prices in California have spiked dramatically compared to the rest of the United States. According to AAA Gas Prices, California's gas prices are currently around $5.50 a gallon for regular gas, $5.90 a gallon for premium gas, and $6.40 a gallon for diesel. Utah gas prices, on the other hand, are currently around $4.10 a gallon for regular gas, $4.50 a gallon for premium gas, and $5.30 a gallon for diesel (AAA Gas Prices). Think about it you can save thousands, yes thousands, of dollars if you drive a fuel-efficient vehicle. According to ChooseEV, consumers can save around $2,850-3,400 a year on gas in California and thus, around $2,000-$2,700 a year on gas in Utah. Think about how much you can save if you drive a fuel-efficient vehicle for 5 years or even 10 years; that's a lot of money you can save.
